about Sofaworld

The sofa is perhaps the most important piece of furniture in one’s home. Not only will members of your family use it daily but it is also the first and most intimate connection that guests will make when visiting your home .It is a statement of personal style .A well-chosen sofa can be the center piece of your living room. The sofa is a vital part of the spiritual DNA of one’s home since it epitomizes the comforts of home and heart.

We at sofaworld understand the importance of a sofa, that is both beautiful and luxurious. Our slogan says it all “Where dreams meet reality“.

The company was founded in February of 2016 by Owner Gabriel Hendricks with his staff has a collective of over 30 experience in lounge suite manufacturing .Sofaworld run the production facility with a attention to detail reserved only for those who love what they are doing while Gabriel dedicates his time to sourcing the best and latest designs in products and fabrics as well as ensuring that his customers are treated in a style fit for royalty.
